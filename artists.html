Artist Profiles Frank I adhere to the philosophy of delicious creation. What inspires me cannot be faked. Without this blood of inspiration flowing through my veins, I can seldom summon the energy to move. Yet, this is the beauty of delicious creation; a daily necessity is transformed into a divine instrument. What goes into me is artwork; what comes out of me is artwork. Delicious creation is a beautiful circle of life. Jones I used to be known as a tough character. It served me well for a while; establishing hard boundaries is a necessity when you’re around the kind of folks I grew up with. Yet, eventually I found myself pounded out into the smooth mold of society; I guess I came here to see if I could get some of my rough edges back. I push boundaries. I refuse to be restrained by arbitrary limitations. Some people find me too rough. Too crass. Too real. I found the line between creation and destruction and I gnawed it to shreds. I’ll bite your toes. Why don’t you bite mine. Nico Crunchy Ankles
